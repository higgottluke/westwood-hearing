About Us

☆

Westwood Hearing Instruments is a family-owned hearing healthcare clinic that's been helping West LA hear better for almost 35 years.

Owned and operated by Della Wolfson, our resident Hearing Instruments Specialist, Westwood Hearing has proudly been a family-run business since its birth in 1987.

Services We Offer

☆

Our friendly team specializes in providing service to our patients who already use assistive hearing devices, and need help with adjustments, cleaning, or repairs. We're also more than happy to help new patients with hearing exams and consultations, and we offer products from the world's leading assitive hearing device manufacturers.

Exams and Consultation

Find out if you or a family member could benefit from an assitive listening device by coming in for a free* consultation and hearing exam.

Repairs

Sometimes, things break. We'll happily do our best to fix your device at our office; and if we can't, we'll send it to our friends who can and get it back to you as quickly as possible.

Check-up and Cleaning

Hearing Aids are expensive devices. Get the longest life out of yours by coming in often for check-ups and cleaning by our trained, professional staff.

Refitting

We want to make sure our patients are happy with their devices, and we're happy to adjust, reprogram, or refit them as often as we need to.
